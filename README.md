For the LIF Project

This application is a project completed by Group B68 of the LIF course, corresponding to our research topic: Developing Healthy Sleep Habits in College Students.
Modern college students generally maintain irregular daily routines, with insufficient sleep duration and poor sleep quality, which exerts a severe impact on their physical and mental health. Through research and analysis, we identified that improper daily time arrangement and unhealthy daily routines are the primary causes of delayed bedtime among college students.
Therefore, we aim to help users manage their time more effectively via this app. Users can review their daily time allocation by recording past schedules, as well as plan ahead for the next day. In this way, individuals can gain better control over their daily arrangements and secure adequate sleep time.

用于 LIF 课程项目

本app为LIF Group B68的小组的项目，服务于我们的选题：Developing Healthy Sleep Habits in College Students.

当代大学生的作息不规律，睡眠的时长与质量难以保证，对身心健康有较大影响。而经过调查与分析，我们发现其睡眠时间拖后的主要原因是一天的时间分配不周，以及不好的作息习惯。

所以，我们希望通过此app来帮助大家更好的规划时间。用户既可以通过记录过去的时间来复盘今天的时间分配，也可以提前规划明天的时间以作计划。如此，我们便能更好地掌握自己的一天，以保证自己的睡眠时间。
